__Search Commands:__

__/t__ or __/title__ = go to title

__/abbr__ = go to abbreviations (if available)

__/f__ or __/first__ = go to first page

__/l__ or __/last__ = go to last page

__/r__ or __/rand__ or __/random__ = go to random page

__/X__ means go to page __X__ where __X__ is any whole number, so __/5__ would take you to page __5__ and __/501__ to page __501__
